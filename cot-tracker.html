Speculative positioning — the contrarian gauge

Specs washed out → mildly constructive

Managed-money longs collapsed into the selloff. At extreme long (euphoria) a top is near; when specs are washed out, the selling is largely spent and a catalyst can spark trend-following buying. We're now in the washed-out half — not a buy signal by itself, but the spec overhang is gone.

The myth-buster: "Wall Street knows the direction" is mostly false. The hedge funds (managed money) are trend-followers and crowd into tops/bottoms — that's why COT is read contrarian. The structurally-right money is commercials (opposite specs) and central banks (price-insensitive, the floor). Watch extremes, not the herd.
